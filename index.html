If you regularly encounter businesses that need cleaner books, better branding, a stronger web presence, or growth strategy, you qualify.
A qualified referral is a net-new business prospect that is not currently a Turnkey client, not in our active pipeline, and fits our ideal customer profile. The referred business must complete our sales process and pay their first invoice for your payout to be triggered.
Payouts are calculated monthly. Once the referred client pays their first invoice and completes a 30-day hold period, your payout is approved and sent. Most partners receive their first payment within 60 days of the referral closing.
No cap. Your earnings scale with the number and size of referrals you send. Whether you refer 2 businesses a year or 20, there is no ceiling on partner income.
Absolutely. If a single business needs CFO services and a new website, you earn on both. Each service line pays independently. Cross-service referrals are the fastest way to increase your annual partner income.
Never. Your only job is the introduction. Turnkey handles all sales conversations, scoping, proposals, onboarding, and service delivery. You stay informed on referral status but never have to negotiate, pitch, or manage anything.
We respect professional conduct rules. If your licensure requires disclosure of referral compensation, we support that fully. Our partner agreement includes written disclosure provisions, and we offer non-cash co-marketing options for partners where direct compensation is restricted.
For project-based work (Web, Design), your payout is locked once the client pays. For recurring services (CFO, SEO), payouts are protected after the hold period. If a refund occurs during the hold window, the payout may be adjusted. If a client cancels their service, partner payouts will discontinue in tandem. Once the hold period clears, any payouts already issued are final.
We send every partner a monthly statement with a full breakdown of your referral income — including referral status, closed deals, payout amounts, and cumulative earnings. You always know exactly where each referral stands and what you’ve earned.
Referral moments happen more often than you think. If you overhear someone starting a business, that’s a huge opportunity — new businesses need books, branding, and a web presence from day one. If a colleague vents about messy financials or tax surprises, ask who handles their books — that’s your cue. When someone says “our website is outdated” or “we don’t show up on Google,” mention TurnkeyWEB or SEO. Even a casual “business is growing fast and I can’t keep up” is an opening — Turnkey CFO helps businesses scale operations.
